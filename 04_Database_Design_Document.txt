1. Introduction
1.1 Purpose
This document defines the database design for Murivest OS, including entity-relationship models, schema definitions, and data management strategies.
1.2 Database Selection
Murivest OS employs a polyglot persistence architecture:
PostgreSQL 15+: Primary operational database for transactional data
MongoDB 6+: Document store for unstructured content
Redis 7+: Caching and session storage
ClickHouse: Analytics warehouse for reporting

Data Retention & Archival
Data retention policies ensure optimal database performance while maintaining compliance:
* Activity logs: 2 years (archive to cold storage after 1 year)
* Audit trails: 7 years (regulatory requirement)
* Deleted records: Soft delete with 90-day grace period
* Session data: 30 days maximum
* Analytics data: Indefinite retention in warehouse
